WHAT IS
PLUM ITV

Read FAQ

Plum iTV is complete, scalable and flexible solution that provides the all modern features. Implementation is very simply since its core elements - Transcoder, Media Engine (Streamers, Load Balancer and NAS), Middleware (CMS and subscriber's applications) and Monitoring are already integrated. Plum iTV includes applications for Android/iOS mobile devices, Android STB, web application, application for Smart TV and possibility for ChromeCast extension for mobile devices.

Every part of our solution is carefully designed and made in accordance to experience and good practices from our previous projects and feedback from our clients and their end-users. There is no such requirement and customization that can’t be done by our in-house R&D team.
